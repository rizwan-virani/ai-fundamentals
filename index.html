How to use this platform

  1. Read the Course Mechanics & Career Guidance cards first. Understand how the course works: how it is scored, the question styles, and where AI Fundamentals sits in your studies and career.
  2. Deep-dive the six Domain Study cards. Each opens a rigorous, scenario-driven reading interface that mirrors a comprehensive prep manual.
  3. Lock in the terms with Rapid Recall Flashcards. 100 cards per domain plus a Master Acronym Drill, powered by spaced repetition — grade each card honestly and the scheduler resurfaces what you're forgetting and retires what you know.
  4. Validate your knowledge with the Domain Quizzes. Ten focused questions per domain, drawn at random and graded with full rationale.
  5. Train practical skills in the PBQ Simulators. 30 interactive performance-based decision scenarios across prompt construction, output evaluation, verification, responsible use, and context selection, plus a drag-and-drop taxonomy engine that has you sort real-world scenarios into the correct categories.
  6. Benchmark readiness with the full-length, timed practice mock. 80 questions across the six domains, with a countdown timer and a scoring dashboard.
  7. Get hands-on in the Labs. Twenty interactive AI-practice labs spanning every domain — flip each card to review its objectives, then launch a full-screen console with guided activities, decision panels, and a session log.
  8. Plan your path in the Supplemental Learning Hub. Open the Industry Certification Explorer to compare 41 industry certifications across 12 vendors, with costs, exam blueprints, DoD 8140 mapping, and salary and role insights.

Notice: This platform is optimized for desktop or laptop computers running the Google Chrome browser. Interactive elements, including the hands-on labs, terminal simulations, and drag-and-drop activities, require a mouse and keyboard to function correctly. These features may not display or operate properly on mobile devices.
